Our Story

We first collaborated as advisors on the construction of the Kingdom City Church in Auckland. Faced with the height of pandemic restrictions, we successfully navigated the project through a landscape of escalating costs and workforce disruptions. This experience instilled a culture of disciplined thinking and proactive problem-solving that remains the cornerstone of our approach today.

Today, we are a growing team of development and project management specialists delivering appraisals, strategy, procurement, and construction monitoring across New Zealand, the UK, Brazil, and Japan. By leveraging trusted local teams and our own hands-on experience, we provide the same quality thinking and commitment to success that defined our very first project together.

Our experience spans the property lifecycle; from initial market analysis and acquisition through planning, design, development management and ongoing management. We approach every project with the discipline of an owner and with the experience of a professional adviser.

By operating as owners and consultants, we maintain an understanding of local market dynamics while applying consistent international standards of governance, reporting and delivery. This dual perspective enables us to identify genuine opportunities, manage risk effectively and create long-term value.
