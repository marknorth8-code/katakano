Midlands, United Kingdom

Why we like this market

The Midlands offers a blend of affordability and growth potential. We expect it to benefit from the Government’s target of building 1.5 million homes this parliament, simply because of its central location.

The UK population is growing and the under-supply of new homes is likely to push rents higher. Investments that hit ‘average’ rental returns with potential to provide new supply ought to do very well.
